Scoom

Scoom is a wave defense first person shooter. Survive as you buy upgrades from Shippy, an e-commerce platform notorious for its shady sellers.

Will you survive, or fall victim to scams?

Our goal of making this game is to teach players how to avoid scammers(In the context of this game you'll have to kill the scammers)

In this project, I've contributed to the inventory system, the power ups & the enemy wave system which

determines the number of enemies that are going to spawn in a wave. The rest of my contribution is on minor

details of the game such as the Health Bar , Main Menu Screen & level design.
